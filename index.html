About Me

Hello there! My name is Suhwan Kim, and I'm a game developer who loves immersive experiences that take players on a journey to new worlds. My journey in game development began over a decade ago when I first played games like GTA and Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2. These games ignited a fire within me to pursue game development as a career, leading me to enroll at DigiPen to learn everything I could about the craft.

During my time at DigiPen, I realized that game development encompasses much more than just writing code. I learned about the countless design elements that need to come together to create an engaging game. Through team projects, I developed my communication skills and discovered the power of collaboration in solving complex problems.

The most important lesson I learned was the value of effective communication with team members. By staying in touch frequently and working together to develop a plan of action, we were able to achieve our goals more efficiently and effectively.

In addition to my love for game development, I have a keen interest in software programming. At DigiPen, I had the opportunity to delve deep into this field and discovered that many of the skills I had developed as a game developer were directly transferable to software programming. I found that my ability to think creatively, problem-solve, and communicate effectively were all essential in software development. I became skilled at writing clean, efficient code and developing software solutions that met users' needs.

As a Graphics, Gameplay, and Software Engineer, I bring all these skills and experiences to my work. I am passionate about creating software that solves real-world problems and enhances the lives of users. Whether it's a game or a software application, I am dedicated to creating functional and enjoyable products.

I'm like a resilient force, thriving in ever-changing environments and adapting with unwavering determination, even when faced with adversity. This outlook fuels my belief that the pursuit of knowledge is an endless journey, filled with opportunities for personal growth and fresh perspectives. By embracing continuous learning, I not only expand my understanding but also keep pushing forward, lighting up my path with progress and newfound wisdom.
